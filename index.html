Portfolio Requirements

You have to pass 2 exams during your training viz:

  1. AKT(Applied Knowledge Test)
  2. SCA(Simulated Consultation Assessment)

AKT Insights

Link to AKT Insights-YouTube Video
  • Tha AKT is a multiple choice exam(Best Answer) in which you have to attempt 200 questions in 3 hours(180minutes).
  • Speed and accuracy are key to passing the exam
  • You need adequate preparation to pass

The breakdown of the AKT sections

  • Clinical Knowledge--80%(ie 160 questions)
  • GP administration(Organisation and Management)--10%(ie 20 questions)
  • Applied Biostatistics(Evidence-based Practice)--10%(ie 20 questions)

The pass mark for the AKT varies: usually ranges from 66-73%(High pass mark!!!)

SCA Insights

  • The SCA is a new exam which would be commencing on the first week of November,2023
  • A remote exam--video and telephone consultations
  • Expected to be similar to previous forms of OSCEs(CSA and RCA)
  • The goal if to improve on your consultation skills while in practice and to form study groups close to your sca
